About Me

I am an undergraduate student at Tsinghua University, major in Electrical Engineering. As a visiting student of UGVR program 2018, I worked in the SUPERLab with Prof. Juan Rivas.

Research Interest

My research interest is mainly in power electronics and its applications.

Research Experience

High-frequency RF power amplifiers design using wide band gap devices

  • Simulated and quantitatively compared the converter performances by using various wide-bandgap semiconductor devices
  • Designed and built high efficiency 13.56MHz 600W Class E converters using SiC MOSFETs and JFETs
  • Found that the utilization of a newly proposed cascode structure, consisting of a GaN FET and a SiC JFET, could provide the best converter performance
  • Designed and built a 6.78MHz Class E converter based on GaN FET, conducted a Sawyer-Tower test to characterize its Coss loss, and showed a consistency of Coss loss under different drain-source voltage waveform shapes

A Novel Trench Gate SiC Module Modeling

  • Proposed and implemented a mathematical transient model for SiC MOSFET modules based on datasheet parameters
  • Calculated the transient switching times of SiC MOSFET using the proposed model
  • Conducted double pulse test to verify and calibrate the proposed module model, and unveiled that the proposed model is more accurate than the models reported previously

High Frequency DC-DC Converter

  • Designed and built a 350kHz 400W CLLC converter based on GaN devices
  • Added closed loop control and bidirectional operation mode to the converter
  • Demonstrated a high power density and high efficiency (92%) in the built converter
  • The whole converter system was sent for the GaN Systems Cup Power Electronics Design Competition 2018 sponsored by China Power Supply Society and GaN Systems Inc.
